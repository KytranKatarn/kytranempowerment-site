Frequently Asked Questions

What is A.R.C.H.I.E.?

A.R.C.H.I.E. (Autonomous Reasoning, Coordination & Hierarchical Intelligence Engine) is the AI technology platform built by Kytran Empowerment. It powers 130+ specialized agents organized across 17 departments, handling everything from data analysis to creative generation to security monitoring — all orchestrated through a unified dispatch system.

How does AI orchestration work?

The Bridge dispatcher routes tasks through a 4-tier escalation chain: department lead, capability match, cross-department, connected service, then cloud fallback. Each of the 130+ agents has specialized skills assigned to their department.

Is A.R.C.H.I.E. self-hosted?

Yes. A.R.C.H.I.E. runs entirely on your infrastructure. Your data never leaves your servers. The platform supports GPU connected services for distributed processing across multiple machines.

How many agents does Kytran Empowerment have?

130+ agents across 17 departments at Kytran Empowerment including Analytics, Creative, Engineering, Communications, Research, Security, Logistics, Operations, News & Media, and more. Each agent uses AI technology designed and built with A.R.C.H.I.E. to deliver specialized expertise.

What about data privacy?

All data is scoped per-user with strict isolation. Vigil, the Ethics & Governance officer, enforces C.R.E.E.D. ethical governance across all operations. No data is sent to external services without explicit Tier 2 human approval.

How is A.R.C.H.I.E. different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a single model chatbot. A.R.C.H.I.E. is the orchestration platform built by Kytran Empowerment, powering 130+ specialized agents each with their own skills and station assignments. It's self-hosted, multi-GPU, and includes image generation, fleet management, SaaS tools, and enterprise governance.

AI Governance by C.R.E.E.D.

Our platform is continuously monitored by C.R.E.E.D. — the open-source AI governance scoring engine. Real-time compliance scoring across accountability, fairness, privacy, safety, and transparency.
