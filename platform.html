How It Works

Three layers of autonomous intelligence working together.

1

Starbase Command Dispatches

The central Starbase Command receives tasks and dispatches them across hub and fleet nodes — each staffed by dedicated AI agents with domain expertise.

2

Multi-GPU Fleet Processing

The Smart Router uses a 4-tier escalation chain across fleet nodes, hub, free cloud, and paid cloud — with hardware-aware model selection matching tasks to the best available GPU.

3

Continuous Self-Improvement

The self-improvement pipeline scans for issues, generates proposals, tests in sandboxed environments, and deploys fixes — continuously upgrading the system.
